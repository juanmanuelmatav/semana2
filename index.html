Juan Manuel Mata

Autobiography

My name is Juan Manuel Mata, I was born in Puerto Plata, on November 3, 1999. I grew up in the city of Punta Cana and graduated from Punta Cana International School in 2017. During my junior and senior years I joined various models of the United Nations like MINUP 2016-2017 and NYDRMUN 2017 and won the award for best delegate in MINUP 2016. Also, I was part of extracurricular activities such as swimming, soccer and music. I am currently studying Visual Communications with concentration in Communication Design, at Altos de Chavn, School of Design.
